NT.gov.au Design System

Design and build consistent, user-focused experiences across NT Government.

Introduction

We have developed a design system for whole-of-government to provide a consistent look and feel across NT Government's digital properties. It is a solid framework that can be tailored to suit the diverse range of sites and applications within the Northern Territory.

The tools help designers and developers build consistent, user-focused experiences for our audiences.

  • Comprehensive component library with WCAG AA coverage
  • Design tokens for color, spacing, typography, and radii
  • React + TypeScript and vanilla JS runtime for CMS

Who is it for?

Built for NT Government teams to reduce time and cost while keeping quality and accessibility high.

Designers

Quickly create wireframes and high-fidelity designs with ready patterns.

Developers

Implement pixel-perfect UIs with React components and strong docs.

Content creators

Compose pages confidently knowing layouts align to production UI.

Product owners

Review consistent experiences and give feedback faster.
